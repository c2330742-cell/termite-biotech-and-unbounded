## Vision

To establish the Philippines as a global leader in termite-based biotechnology, developing sustainable solutions for renewable energy, natural pest management, and innovative agricultural practices.

## Three Interconnected Research Projects

### 1. Biofuel Research (₱23M)
- **Objective:** Develop commercially viable bioethanol from termite gut microbiomes
- **Innovation:** Novel enzyme pathways from termite cellulose digestion
- **Revenue Potential:** ₱260M over Years 3-5
- **Market:** Alternative fuel, industrial ethanol, pharmaceutical intermediates

### 2. Natural Pest Control (₱3M)
- **Objective:** Create eco-friendly termite-derived pest management solutions
- **Innovation:** Bioactive compounds disrupting pest reproductive cycles
- **Revenue Potential:** ₱37M over Years 2-4
- **Market:** Organic agriculture, urban pest control, export markets

### 3. Termite Farming (₱3.2M)
- **Objective:** Establish sustainable termite farming systems for raw material supply
- **Innovation:** Controlled breeding protocols optimizing compound production
- **Revenue Potential:** ₱22M over Years 2-4
- **Market:** Research supply, pharmaceutical precursors, animal feed

## Why Invest Now?

2. **Global Trend:** Bio-economy market projected to reach ₱18 trillion by 2030
3. **Government Support:** DOST-PCAARRD priority funding area for agricultural biotechnology
