## 1. RISK ASSESSMENT OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Risk Rating Scale

| Likelihood | Score | Description |
|------------|-------|-------------|
| Rare | 1 | <10% chance of occurring |
| Unlikely | 2 | 10-30% chance |
| Possible | 3 | 30-50% chance |
| Likely | 4 | 50-70% chance |
| Almost Certain | 5 | >70% chance |

| Impact | Score | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Negligible | 1 | Minimal effect on project |
| Minor | 2 | <10% budget or schedule impact |
| Moderate | 3 | 10-25% budget or schedule impact |
| Major | 4 | 25-50% budget or schedule impact |
| Catastrophic | 5 | >50% budget or schedule impact |

### 1.2 Risk Score Calculation

**Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact**

| Score Range | Risk Level | Response |
|-------------|------------|----------|
| 1-4 | Low | Monitor and document |
| 5-9 | Medium | Active monitoring, contingency plan |
| 10-15 | High | Immediate mitigation required |
| 16-25 | Critical | Project pause, executive review |

## 8. RISK RESPONSE PLANS

### 8.1 Critical Risk Response: T02 - Termite Gut Microbiome Not Culturable

**Risk Score:** 16 (Critical)

**Trigger:** Unable to culture >30% of identified bacteria within 6 months

**Response Plan:**

**Immediate Actions (Week 1-2):**
1. Assemble emergency technical review team
2. Document all failed culture attempts
3. Consult external experts in gut microbiome research
4. Review literature for alternative approaches

**Short-term Mitigation (Month 1-3):**
1. Invest in metagenomic sequencing (₱500,000)
2. Partner with international labs specializing in unculturable bacteria
3. Explore synthetic biology approaches to express genes in culturable hosts
4. Screen existing culture collections for similar organisms

**Medium-term Adaptation (Month 3-6):**
1. Shift focus to metagenomics-based enzyme discovery
2. Develop cell-free expression systems
3. Explore horizontal gene transfer from termite microbiome
4. Consider alternative termite species with culturable microbiomes

**Contingency (Month 6+):**
1. Pivot to termite-derived compounds (non-microbial)
2. License enzymes from other institutions
3. Focus on farming and pest control projects
4. Consider acquiring technology from competitors

**Budget Impact:** ₱2,000,000 additional investment  
**Schedule Impact:** 6-9 month delay  
**Decision Point:** Month 3 review
