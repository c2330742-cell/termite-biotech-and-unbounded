## PRESENTATION TIPS

### Delivery Guidelines

**Timing:**
- Introduction: 2 minutes
- Problem/Solution: 4 minutes
- Market/Tech: 4 minutes
- Business Model: 3 minutes
- Financials: 3 minutes
- Team/Ask: 3 minutes
- Q&A: 10 minutes

**Key Messages to Emphasize:**
1. First-mover advantage in Philippine bio-economy
2. Strong IP protection and government support
3. Multiple revenue streams with high ROI
4. Experienced team with proven track record
5. Significant social and environmental impact

**Common Questions to Prepare:**
1. How do you plan to scale production?
2. What are your key technical risks?
3. How do you protect your IP?
4. What's your path to profitability?
5. Why should we invest now?

**Visual Best Practices:**
- Use high-quality images and graphics
- Keep text minimal (6 lines max per slide)
- Use consistent color scheme and branding
- Include data visualizations for numbers
- Leave white space for readability
