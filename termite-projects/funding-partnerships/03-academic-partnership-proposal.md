## 1. ABOUT THE INITIATIVE

### 1.1 Project Overview

The Integrated Termite Research Initiative is a flagship program developing Philippine termite biotechnology for:
- **Biofuel production** (₱23M budget)
- **Natural pest control** (₱3M budget)  
- **Sustainable farming systems** (₱3.2M budget)

### 1.2 Why Termite Research?

Termite gut microbiomes contain unique enzymatic capabilities that can:
- Reduce biofuel production costs by 40-60%
- Provide alternatives to synthetic pesticides
- Create new agricultural value chains
- Generate significant export revenue
