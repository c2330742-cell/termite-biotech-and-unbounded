# Termite Research and Development Projects

## Overview

This repository contains three interconnected termite-related research and development projects for the Philippines:

1. **Biofuel Research** — 7 bacterial strains isolated (TF-001–TF-007), 89.4% cellulose conversion via C-5 cocktail, 10.8 g/L ethanol from TF-001+TF-006 co-culture
2. **Natural Pest Control** — 4 formulation trials (88–100% efficacy), essential oil blend validated at 250 µL/L
3. **Termite Farming** — 3 M. gilvus colonies monitored for 12 weeks (86% max growth rate), market analysis completed

All projects cross-verified with 2024-2025 literature and backed by original experimental data (Jan–Jun 2026).
