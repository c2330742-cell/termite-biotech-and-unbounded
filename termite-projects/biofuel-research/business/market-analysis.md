## Competitive Analysis

### Current Biofuel Technologies

**First-Generation Biofuels**:
- Corn-based bioethanol (USA)
- Sugarcane-based bioethanol (Brazil)
- Palm oil-based biodiesel (Malaysia, Indonesia)

**Advantages**:
- Established technology
- Large-scale production
- Government support

**Disadvantages**:
- Food vs. fuel debate
- Land use concerns
- Water consumption
- Limited sustainability

**Second-Generation Biofuels**:
- Cellulosic ethanol (from agricultural waste)
- Algae-based biofuels
- Waste-to-energy technologies

**Advantages**:
- No food competition
- Better sustainability
- Waste utilization
- Carbon neutrality

**Disadvantages**:
- Higher production costs
- Technical challenges
- Limited commercialization
