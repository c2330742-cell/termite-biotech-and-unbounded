## Enzyme Production

### 1. Fermentation Conditions

**Submerged Fermentation**:
- Medium: Cellulose-based medium
- Temperature: 30-35°C
- pH: 6.5-7.0
- Agitation: 150-200 rpm
- Duration: 48-72 hours

**Solid-State Fermentation**:
- Substrate: Agricultural residues
- Moisture content: 60-70%
- Temperature: 30-35°C
- Duration: 5-7 days

### 2. Purification Methods

**Ammonium Sulfate Precipitation**:
- 30-70% saturation
- Dialysis against buffer
- Desalting by gel filtration

**Ion Exchange Chromatography**:
- DEAE-Sepharose or CM-Sepharose
- Linear salt gradient
- Collect active fractions

**Gel Filtration Chromatography**:
- Sephadex G-75 or G-100
- Buffer elution
- Polishing step

### 3. Characterization Methods

**SDS-PAGE**:
- Molecular weight determination
- Purity assessment

**Isoelectric Focusing**:
- pI determination
- Charge heterogeneity

**Mass Spectrometry**:
- Exact molecular weight
- Peptide mapping
- Post-translational modifications

## Quality Control

### Activity Assays

**Cellulase Assay (DNS Method)**:
- Substrate: CMC
- Temperature: 50°C
- Time: 30 minutes
- Detection: DNS reagent, 540 nm

**β-Glucosidase Assay**:
- Substrate: p-nitrophenyl-β-D-glucopyranoside
- Temperature: 50°C
- Time: 10 minutes
- Detection: 405 nm

**Xylanase Assay**:
- Substrate: Birchwood xylan
- Temperature: 50°C
- Time: 10 minutes
- Detection: DNS reagent, 540 nm

### Stability Testing

**Thermal Stability**:
- Incubate at various temperatures
- Measure residual activity
- Determine half-life

**pH Stability**:
- Incubate at various pH values
- Measure residual activity
- Determine optimal pH range

**Storage Stability**:
- Store at various conditions
- Measure activity over time
- Determine shelf life

## Safety Considerations

### Handling Precautions
- Wear gloves and lab coat
- Avoid inhalation of enzyme powders
- Work in fume hood for concentrated solutions
- Use appropriate PPE

### Waste Disposal
- Autoclave enzyme solutions before disposal
- Follow institutional guidelines
- Document disposal

### Emergency Procedures
- Eye contact: Flush with water for 15 minutes
- Skin contact: Wash with soap and water
- Inhalation: Move to fresh air
- Ingestion: Seek medical attention
