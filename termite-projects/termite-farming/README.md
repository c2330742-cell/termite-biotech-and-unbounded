## Components

### 1. Research & Planning
- Study termite farming methods (Thailand, Africa)
- Identify suitable local species (Macrotermes, Coptotermes)
- Legal requirements and permits
- Market analysis for termite products

### 2. Farm Setup
- **Enclosure Design**
  - Outdoor mounds (simulated natural habitat)
  - Indoor controlled environment
  - Climate control systems

  - **Breeding Stock**
    - Colony collection from wild
    - Initial breeding pairs
    - Colony management protocols

### 3. Production Systems
- **Fungus Gardens** (Macrotermes species)
  - Fungus comb cultivation
  - Substrate preparation
  - Harvesting techniques

  - **Direct Harvesting**
    - Worker termite collection
    - Soldier termite separation
    - Eggs and larvae harvest

### 4. Processing & Products
- **Food Products**
  - Dried termite snacks
  - Termite flour (protein powder)
  - Termite oil extraction
  - Specialty products (wine, supplements)

  - **Animal Feed**
    - Poultry feed supplement
    - Fish feed
    - Pet food ingredients

  - **Biotechnology**
    - Cellulase enzyme extraction
    - Gut bacteria cultivation
    - Research specimens

### 5. Quality Control
- Food safety standards (FDA Philippines)
- Nutritional analysis
- Contamination prevention
- Shelf-life testing

### 6. Marketing & Sales
- Target markets:
  - Local restaurants and food processors
  - Animal feed manufacturers
  - Biotech companies
  - Export markets

- Sales channels:
  - Direct to consumers
  - Wholesale to distributors
  - Online platforms
  - Farmers markets

### 7. Sustainability
- Colony management for long-term production
- Environmental impact assessment
- Waste management
- Carbon footprint reduction

## Deliverables
- Farm infrastructure
- Breeding protocols
- Processing facility
- Product lines
- Sales platform
- Training materials

## Colony Monitoring (12 weeks, Jan–Mar 2026)
Three M. gilvus colonies (MGV-01, MGV-02, MGV-03) collected from Southern Luzon, Philippines on 2026-01-06 and monitored under controlled conditions (28°C, 85% RH):

| Metric | MGV-01 | MGV-02 | MGV-03 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Initial workers | 3,420 | 2,890 | 4,100 |
| Week 12 workers | 5,380 | 4,010 | 7,620 |
| Growth rate | 4.3%/wk | 2.9%/wk | 5.8%/wk |
| Queen fertile | ✅ (Week 5) | No eggs | ✅ (Week 5) |
| Survival | 100% | 100% | 100% |

Full data: `research/colony-monitoring-data.md`
