## Competitive Landscape

### Philippine Insect Protein Producers
| Company | Product | Scale | Price |
|---------|---------|-------|-------|
| Entomo Farm | Cricket powder | 10 tons/yr | ₱1,200/kg |
| Grilo Protein | Cricket flour | 5 tons/yr | ₱950/kg |
| FlyFeed | BSF larvae meal | 50 tons/yr | ₱85/kg |
| **Termite project** | Termite meal | **Target: 50 tons/yr** | **₱150-200/kg** |

### Competitive Advantages
1. Higher protein content than BSF larvae (60-70% vs 40-45%)
2. Complete amino acid profile (comparable to fishmeal)
3. Lower water requirement than cricket farming
4. Natural association with Termitomyces fungus (unique selling point)
5. Integration with biofuel research (enzyme discovery pipeline)
