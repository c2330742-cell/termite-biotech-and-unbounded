## Key Observations
1. **Colony establishment**: All 3 colonies established within 72 h of introduction
2. **Queen fertility**: MGV-01 and MGV-03 queens confirmed fertile (egg production by Week 5)
3. **Fungus comb growth**: Correlated with worker population (r = 0.94)
4. **Optimal conditions**: 28°C, 85% RH supported sustained growth
5. **Feeding rate**: 1 pine wood block (5×5×2 cm) per 1,000 workers per week
6. **Survival rate**: 100% survival for all colonies over 12 weeks

## Recommendations for Scale-Up
1. Target colony size of 5,000+ workers for optimal egg production
2. Maintain humidity at 85 ± 5% — critical for fungus comb health
3. Monitor queen health weekly — key predictor of colony growth
4. Pre-sterilize substrate and feed to prevent pathogen introduction
5. Expected harvest yield: ~8 g termite biomass per 1,000 workers per month
