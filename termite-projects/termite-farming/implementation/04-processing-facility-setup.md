# Processing Facility Setup Guide for Termite Farming

## Executive Summary
This guide provides detailed specifications for setting up a termite processing facility, including equipment requirements, workflow design, food safety protocols, and production capacity planning. Modeled after Thai operations with adaptations for modern food processing standards.

## 1. Facility Requirements

### 1.1 Space Requirements
**Minimum Processing Area:** 2,000-3,000 sq ft
- Receiving/Unloading: 300 sq ft
- Cleaning/Sorting: 400 sq ft
- Drying/Processing: 600 sq ft
- Milling/Extraction: 300 sq ft
- Packaging: 400 sq ft
- Cold Storage: 200 sq ft
- Laboratory/QC: 150 sq ft
- Sanitation: 150 sq ft

**Support Areas:**
- Staff facilities: 200 sq ft
- Chemical storage: 100 sq ft
- Waste disposal: 100 sq ft
- Maintenance: 100 sq ft

### 1.2 Utility Requirements
**Electrical:**
- Service: 400A, 3-phase, 480V
- Dedicated circuits for major equipment
- Emergency generator: 150 kW minimum
- UPS for critical systems

**Water:**
- Supply: 5,000 gallons/day minimum
- Hot water: 150°F minimum for sanitization
- Pressure: 40-60 psi
- Drainage: 4-inch main lines

**Ventilation:**
- Exhaust: 20 air changes/hour in processing areas
- Makeup air: Filtered and temperature controlled
- Local exhaust: At drying and milling stations

### 1.3 Construction Specifications
**Flooring:**
- Material: Epoxy-coated concrete
- Finish: Non-slip, food-grade
- Slope: 1-2% toward drains
- Coving: 4-inch radius at wall junctions

**Walls:**
- Material: Fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP) or stainless steel
- Height: To 8 ft minimum
- Finish: Smooth, cleanable, non-porous
- Corners: Rounded for cleaning

**Ceiling:**
- Material: Washable panels
- Height: 10-12 ft minimum
- Insulation: R-30 minimum
- Condensation prevention: Vapor barrier

## 4. Product Specifications

### 4.1 Whole Dried Termites
**Physical Characteristics:**
- Color: Light brown to dark brown
- Texture: Crisp, brittle when properly dried
- Odor: Mild, nutty aroma
- Size: Uniform (sorted by size grade)

**Chemical Specifications:**
- Moisture content: <8%
- Fat content: 20-30%
- Protein content: 40-50%
- Ash content: <5%
- Shelf life: 12 months (proper storage)

**Grades:**
- Grade A: Uniform size, no damage, premium appearance
- Grade B: Mixed sizes, minor damage, standard quality
- Grade C: Irregular sizes, for animal feed only

### 4.2 Termite Flour/Protein Powder
**Physical Characteristics:**
- Color: Light tan to brown
- Texture: Fine powder (200 mesh)
- Odor: Mild, protein-like
- Solubility: Partial in water

**Chemical Specifications:**
- Moisture content: <6%
- Protein content: 55-65%
- Fat content: 15-25%
- Fiber content: <5%
- Particle size: 90% < 75 microns

**Grades:**
- Food Grade: Meets FDA standards for human consumption
- Feed Grade: Meets AAFCO standards for animal feed
- Technical Grade: For industrial/biotech applications

### 4.3 Termite Oil
**Physical Characteristics:**
- Color: Light yellow to golden
- Appearance: Clear, no sediment
- Odor: Mild, characteristic
- Viscosity: Low (similar to vegetable oil)

**Chemical Specifications:**
- Free fatty acid: <1%
- Peroxide value: <10 meq/kg
- Moisture: <0.5%
- Iodine value: 80-120
- Shelf life: 18 months (proper storage)

**Grades:**
- Food Grade: Meets FDA standards
- Cosmetic Grade: Meets USP standards
- Technical Grade: For industrial use

## 5. Food Safety & Quality Control

### 5.1 HACCP Plan Overview
**Critical Control Points (CCPs):**
1. **Receiving** - Temperature check (<40°F)
2. **Cooking/Blanching** - Time/temperature (85°C, 3 min)
3. **Drying** - Moisture reduction (<8%)
4. **Metal Detection** - Physical hazard detection
5. **Packaging** - Seal integrity verification

### 5.2 Prerequisite Programs
**Sanitation SOPs:**
- Equipment cleaning schedules
- Facility cleaning procedures
- Personal hygiene requirements
- Pest control program
- Water safety program

**Operational Programs:**
- Supplier approval program
- Allergen control program
- Chemical control program
- Traceability/recall program
- Employee training program

### 5.3 Monitoring & Verification
**Daily Monitoring:**
- CCP parameter checks (hourly)
- Environmental monitoring
- Equipment calibration
- Employee compliance

**Weekly Verification:**
- Product testing (moisture, microbial)
- Sanitation verification
- Record review
- Corrective action tracking

**Monthly/Quarterly:**
- Internal audits
- Product shelf life testing
- Supplier evaluations
- Regulatory compliance review
