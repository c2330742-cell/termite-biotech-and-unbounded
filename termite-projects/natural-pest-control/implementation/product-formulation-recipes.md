# Natural Termite Pest Control Project
## Product Formulation Recipes

**Target Efficacy:** 100% mortality at 250 µL/L
**Base Active Ingredients:** Clove oil (Eugenol), Cinnamon oil (Cinnamaldehyde)

---

## FORMULA 1: CONCENTRATED TERMITE CONTROL SOLUTION

### Active Ingredients
| Ingredient | Chemical Name | Purity | Concentration | Function |
|------------|---------------|--------|---------------|----------|
| Clove Oil | Eugenol | ≥85% | 150 µL/L | Primary termiticide |
| Cinnamon Oil | Cinnamaldehyde | ≥95% | 100 µL/L | Secondary termiticide |
| Neem Oil | Azadirachtin | ≥10% | 50 µL/L | Growth regulator |

### Inert Ingredients
| Ingredient | Purpose | Concentration |
|------------|---------|---------------|
| Ethanol (food grade) | Solubilizer | 5% v/v |
| Polysorbate 80 | Emulsifier | 2% v/v |
| Citric Acid | pH adjuster | 0.5% w/v |
| Distilled Water | Carrier | q.s. to 100% |
| BHT (Butylated Hydroxytoluene) | Preservative | 0.1% w/v |

### Final Product Specifications
| Parameter | Specification | Test Method |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Appearance | Clear to slightly amber liquid | Visual |
| pH | 5.5 - 6.5 | pH meter |
| Specific Gravity | 0.95 - 1.05 | Hydrometer |
| Viscosity | 10 - 50 cP | Brookfield viscometer |
| Flash Point | >60°C | Cleveland open cup |
| Shelf Life | 24 months | Accelerated stability |

### Manufacturing Procedure
1. **Phase A - Oil Phase:** Combine clove oil, cinnamon oil, neem oil, and ethanol in mixing tank at 25°C
2. **Phase B - Emulsification:** Add polysorbate 80 to Phase A while mixing at 200 RPM
3. **Phase C - Aqueous Phase:** Dissolve citric acid in distilled water
4. **Combination:** Slowly add Phase C to Phase A/B mixture while maintaining 200 RPM
5. **Preservation:** Add BHT and mix for 15 minutes
6. **Quality Check:** Test pH, density, and appearance
7. **Filling:** Package in HDPE containers

## LABORATORY TEST RESULTS (2026)

### Trial 1: Neem Oil Repellent (2026-03-10)
- **Batch Size**: 10 L
- **Formulation**: Neem oil 3%, Tween-80 1.5%, water 95.5%
- **Efficacy Test**: 88% repellency against C. gestroi in choice-chamber assay (n=5)
- **Stability**: Stable emulsion for 8 weeks at room temperature
- **pH**: 6.8
- **Shelf Life Projection**: 10 months

### Trial 2: Essential Oil Blend (2026-03-20)
- **Batch Size**: 5 L
- **Formulation**: Clove oil 2.5%, cinnamon oil 1.5%, tea tree oil 1%, Tween-80 2%, water 93%
- **Efficacy Test**: 100% mortality at 250 µL/L (filter paper contact assay, 24 h exposure)
- **Stability**: Emulsion stable for 6 weeks; phase separation observed at week 8
- **pH**: 6.2
- **Shelf Life Projection**: 6-8 months

### Trial 3: Borate Bait Granules (2026-04-01)
- **Batch Size**: 5 kg
- **Formulation**: Borax 25%, diatomaceous earth 45%, cellulose powder 15%, molasses 10%, binder 5%
- **Efficacy Test**: 92% mortality in no-choice feeding assay (14 days)
- **Moisture Content**: 6.8%
- **Shelf Life Projection**: 14 months in sealed packaging

### Trial 4: Nematode Suspension (2026-04-15)
- **Batch Size**: 2 L
- **Formulation**: Steinernema carpocapsae at 2 × 10⁶/mL in water with 0.1% surfactant
- **Efficacy Test**: 76% termite mortality in soil bioassay (7 days)
- **Viability**: 88% viability after 2 weeks at 8°C
- **Shelf Life**: 3-4 weeks refrigerated
