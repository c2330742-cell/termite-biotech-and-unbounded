# Safety Data Sheets (SDS) for Natural Termite Control Products

## 1. Neem Oil-Based Repellent

### Product Identification
- **Product Name**: NeemGuard Termite Repellent
- **Chemical Name**: Neem oil emulsion
- **Chemical Formula**: C35H44O16 (Azadirachtin)
- **CAS Number**: 11141-13-2

### Hazard Identification
- **GHS Classification**: Not classified as hazardous
- **Signal Word**: Warning
- **Hazard Statements**: May cause mild skin irritation
- **Precautionary Statements**: Avoid contact with skin and eyes

### First Aid Measures
- **Inhalation**: Move to fresh air if symptoms occur
- **Skin Contact**: Wash with soap and water
- **Eye Contact**: Flush with water for 15 minutes
- **Ingestion**: Rinse mouth, do not induce vomiting

### Fire Fighting Measures
- **Extinguishing Media**: Water, foam, dry chemical
- **Special Hazards**: Not flammable under normal conditions
- **Protective Equipment**: Self-contained breathing apparatus

### Handling and Storage
- **Handling**: Use with adequate ventilation
- **Storage**: Store in cool, dry place away from sunlight
- **Storage Temperature**: 5-25°C

### Toxicological Information
- **Acute Toxicity**: Low toxicity (LD50 >5000 mg/kg)
- **Skin Irritation**: Mild irritation possible
- **Eye Irritation**: Mild irritation possible

### Ecological Information
- **Ecotoxicity**: Low toxicity to aquatic organisms
- **Persistence**: Biodegradable
- **Bioaccumulation**: Low potential

## 2. Citrus-Based Repellent

### Product Identification
- **Product Name**: CitrusShield Termite Repellent
- **Chemical Name**: D-limonene emulsion
- **Chemical Formula**: C10H16
- **CAS Number**: 5989-27-5

### Hazard Identification
- **GHS Classification**: Flammable liquid, Category 4
- **Signal Word**: Warning
- **Hazard Statements**: May cause skin irritation, Flammable liquid
- **Precautionary Statements**: Keep away from heat, Avoid contact with skin

### First Aid Measures
- **Inhalation**: Move to fresh air, seek medical attention if symptoms persist
- **Skin Contact**: Wash with soap and water, remove contaminated clothing
- **Eye Contact**: Flush with water for 15 minutes, seek medical attention
- **Ingestion**: Rinse mouth, seek medical attention

### Fire Fighting Measures
- **Extinguishing Media**: Foam, dry chemical, CO2
- **Special Hazards**: Flammable liquid, vapors may form explosive mixture with air
- **Protective Equipment**: Self-contained breathing apparatus, protective clothing

### Handling and Storage
- **Handling**: Use with adequate ventilation, avoid ignition sources
- **Storage**: Store in cool, dry place away from heat and ignition sources
- **Storage Temperature**: 5-25°C

### Physical and Chemical Properties
- **Appearance**: Clear to pale yellow liquid
- **Odor**: Citrus odor
- **pH**: 6.0-7.5
- **Boiling Point**: 176°C
- **Flash Point**: 48°C (closed cup)
- **Solubility**: Slightly soluble in water

### Toxicological Information
- **Acute Toxicity**: Low toxicity (LD50 >5000 mg/kg)
- **Skin Irritation**: May cause irritation
- **Eye Irritation**: May cause serious irritation

### Ecological Information
- **Ecotoxicity**: Low toxicity to aquatic organisms
- **Persistence**: Readily biodegradable
- **Bioaccumulation**: Low potential

## 4. Entomopathogenic Nematodes

### Product Identification
- **Product Name**: NemaGuard Termite Control
- **Active Ingredient**: Steinernema carpocapsae
- **Formulation**: Aqueous suspension

### Hazard Identification
- **GHS Classification**: Not classified as hazardous
- **Signal Word**: Warning
- **Hazard Statements**: May cause allergic reactions in sensitive individuals
- **Precautionary Statements**: Avoid inhalation of spray mist

### First Aid Measures
- **Inhalation**: Move to fresh air if symptoms occur
- **Skin Contact**: Wash with soap and water
- **Eye Contact**: Flush with water for 15 minutes
- **Ingestion**: Rinse mouth, seek medical attention if symptoms persist

### Storage and Handling
- **Storage**: Store at 4-10°C, do not freeze
- **Shelf Life**: 2-4 weeks when refrigerated
- **Application**: Apply to moist soil in evening or cloudy weather

### Environmental Information
- **Ecotoxicity**: Non-toxic to humans, pets, and beneficial insects
- **Persistence**: Self-sustaining in soil
- **Target Pests**: Subterranean termites only

## General Safety Precautions

### Personal Protective Equipment
- Gloves when handling products
- Safety glasses or goggles
- Mask when mixing or applying sprays
- Protective clothing for large applications

### Environmental Precautions
- Do not apply near water sources
- Avoid contamination of waterways
- Protect beneficial insects
- Follow label instructions

### Storage Guidelines
- Store in original containers
- Keep out of reach of children
- Store in cool, dry place
- Do not contaminate food or feed

### Disposal Guidelines
- Dispose of empty containers properly
- Do not reuse containers
- Follow local disposal regulations
- Rinse containers before disposal
