# Product Formulations for Natural Termite Control

## 1. Botanical Spray Solutions

### 1.1 Neem-Based Repellent

**Ingredients**:
- Neem oil (Azadirachta indica): 2-5%
- Emulsifier (lecithin or Tween-80): 1-2%
- Water: 93-97%
- Optional: Citrus oil (0.5-1%) for enhanced effect

**Preparation**:
1. Mix neem oil with emulsifier
2. Slowly add water while stirring
3. Homogenize using high-shear mixer
4. Package in dark containers (UV sensitive)
5. Store at cool temperature

**Application**:
- Dilute 1:10 with water before use
- Spray on soil around foundation
- Apply every 3-6 months
- Reapply after heavy rain

**Efficacy**: 85-90% repellency for 3-6 months

### 1.2 Citrus-Based Repellent

**Ingredients**:
- Citrus oil (d-limonene): 5-10%
- Emulsifier: 2-3%
- Water: 87-93%
- Preservative (optional): 0.1-0.5%

**Preparation**:
1. Mix citrus oil with emulsifier
2. Gradually add water
3. Mix thoroughly
4. Filter if necessary
5. Package in airtight containers

**Application**:
- Dilute 1:5 with water
- Spray on wood surfaces
- Apply to soil around foundation
- Reapply every 2-3 months

**Efficacy**: 70-80% repellency for 2-3 months

### 1.3 Essential Oil Blend

**Ingredients**:
- Clove oil (eugenol): 2-3%
- Cinnamon oil (cinnamaldehyde): 1-2%
- Tea tree oil: 1-2%
- Emulsifier: 2-3%
- Water: 90-94%

**Preparation**:
1. Blend essential oils together
2. Mix with emulsifier
3. Add water gradually
4. Mix until uniform
5. Package in dark glass bottles

**Application**:
- Dilute 1:10 with water
- Spray on infested areas
- Apply to wood surfaces
- Reapply monthly

**Efficacy**: 75-85% repellency for 1-2 months

## 2. Granular Products

### 2.1 Borate Granules

**Ingredients**:
- Borax (sodium borate): 20-30%
- Diatomaceous earth: 40-50%
- Attractant (sugar or cellulose): 10-15%
- Binding agent: 5-10%
- Colorant (optional): 0.1-0.5%

**Preparation**:
1. Mix borax with diatomaceous earth
2. Add attractant and mix
3. Add binding agent
4. Granulate using extrusion or prilling
5. Dry to 5-10% moisture content
6. Package in moisture-proof bags

**Application**:
- Apply 100-200g per square meter
- Place around foundation perimeter
- Apply in garden beds
- Reapply annually

**Efficacy**: 90-95% mortality for 12+ months

### 2.2 Diatomaceous Earth Granules

**Ingredients**:
- Diatomaceous earth (food grade): 70-80%
- Attractant (cellulose powder): 10-20%
- Binding agent: 5-10%
- Repellent oil: 1-2%

**Preparation**:
1. Mix diatomaceous earth with attractant
2. Add binding agent
3. Add repellent oil and mix
4. Granulate and dry
5. Package in sealed containers

**Application**:
- Apply 50-100g per square meter
- Use in wall voids and crawl spaces
- Apply around foundation
- Reapply every 6-12 months

**Efficacy**: 80-90% mortality through desiccation

## 3. Bait Systems

### 3.1 Cellulose-Based Bait

**Ingredients**:
- Cellulose powder: 60-70%
- Sugar or molasses: 10-15%
- Borate or IGR (Insect Growth Regulator): 1-5%
- Binding agent: 5-10%
- Water: 10-15%

**Preparation**:
1. Mix cellulose with sugar/molasses
2. Add borate or IGR
3. Add binding agent and water
4. Form into blocks or granules
5. Package in bait stations

**Application**:
- Place bait stations every 10-15 feet
- Position near termite activity
- Monitor and replace as needed
- Maintain for 12+ months

**Efficacy**: 85-95% colony elimination with IGR

### 3.2 Paper-Based Bait

**Ingredients**:
- Shredded paper or cardboard: 70-80%
- Sugar solution: 10-15%
- Borate: 2-5%
- Water: 5-10%

**Preparation**:
1. Shred paper/cardboard
2. Soak in sugar solution
3. Mix with borate
4. Place in bait stations
5. Keep moist

**Application**:
- Place in areas of termite activity
- Check weekly for activity
- Replace when consumed
- Use as monitoring tool

**Efficacy**: 70-80% for monitoring and small colonies

## 4. Wood Treatment Products

### 4.1 Borate Wood Preservative

**Ingredients**:
- Borax: 10-15%
- Boric acid: 5-10%
- Water: 75-85%
- Optional: Glycol carrier (5-10%)

**Preparation**:
1. Dissolve borax in hot water
2. Add boric acid and stir
3. Cool and filter
4. Package in sealed containers
5. Store at room temperature

**Application**:
- Apply by brushing, spraying, or dipping
- Treat bare wood only
- Apply 2-3 coats
- Allow to dry between coats

**Efficacy**: 95-100% protection for 20+ years

### 4.2 Copper-Based Preservative

**Ingredients**:
- Copper sulfate: 5-10%
- Borax: 5-10%
- Water: 80-90%
- Surfactant: 0.1-0.5%

**Preparation**:
1. Dissolve copper sulfate in water
2. Add borax and stir
3. Add surfactant
4. Filter and package
5. Store in non-metallic containers

**Application**:
- Apply by brushing or spraying
- Treat bare wood only
- Apply 2-3 coats
- Allow to dry thoroughly

**Efficacy**: 90-95% protection for 15+ years

## 5. Soil Treatment Products

### 5.1 Nematode Suspension

**Ingredients**:
- Entomopathogenic nematodes (Steinernema or Heterorhabditis): 1-5 million/mL
- Water: 95-99%
- Protectant (optional): 0.1-0.5%

**Preparation**:
1. Obtain nematode culture
2. Dilute to desired concentration
3. Add protectant if needed
4. Package in breathable containers
5. Store at 4-10°C

**Application**:
- Apply with watering can or sprayer
- Apply to moist soil
- Apply in evening or cloudy weather
- Reapply every 3-6 months

**Efficacy**: 70-85% termite mortality

### 5.2 Fungal Spore Suspension

**Ingredients**:
- Metarhizium or Beauveria spores: 10^6-10^8/mL
- Water: 90-99%
- Adhesive (optional): 0.1-0.5%

**Preparation**:
1. Produce spores in fermentation
2. Concentrate by filtration or centrifugation
3. Suspend in water with adhesive
4. Package in breathable containers
5. Store at 4-10°C

**Application**:
- Apply with sprayer
- Apply to soil and wood surfaces
- Apply in humid conditions
- Reapply every 2-3 months

**Efficacy**: 60-80% termite mortality

## Storage and Handling

### Liquid Products
- Store in cool, dry place
- Protect from sunlight
- Keep containers sealed
- Shelf life: 6-12 months

### Granular Products
- Store in dry conditions
- Protect from moisture
- Keep in sealed containers
- Shelf life: 12-24 months

### Bait Products
- Store at room temperature
- Keep moist until use
- Replace when consumed
- Shelf life: 3-6 months

## Safety Considerations

### Personal Protective Equipment
- Gloves when handling
- Safety glasses
- Mask when mixing
- Protective clothing

### Environmental Precautions
- Avoid water contamination
- Do not apply near water sources
- Protect beneficial insects
- Follow label instructions

### First Aid
- Skin contact: Wash with soap and water
- Eye contact: Flush with water
- Ingestion: Seek medical attention
- Inhalation: Move to fresh air
