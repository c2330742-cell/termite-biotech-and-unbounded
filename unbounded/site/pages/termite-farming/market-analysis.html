Competitive Advantages

  1. Higher protein content than BSF larvae (60-70% vs 40-45%)
  2. Complete amino acid profile (comparable to fishmeal)
  3. Lower water requirement than cricket farming
  4. Natural association with Termitomyces fungus (unique selling point)
  5. Integration with biofuel research (enzyme discovery pipeline)
