Components

1. Research Phase

  • Study local termite species and behavior
  • Identify natural predators and pathogens
  • Research botanical repellents and toxins
  • Analyze successful case studies from other countries

2. Natural Control Methods

  • Biological Control
  • Nematodes (Steinernema, Heterorhabditis)
  • Fungal pathogens (Metarhizium, Beauveria)

  • Predatory insects (ant species)

  • Botanical Repellents

    • Neem oil extract
    • Citrus-based solutions
    • Essential oils (clove, cinnamon, tea tree)

  • Physical Barriers

    • Sand barriers
    • Steel mesh
    • Borate-treated materials

3. Product Development

  • Liquid Solutions: Botanical sprays for soil treatment
  • Granular Products: Slow-release repellents
  • Bait Systems: Natural bait stations
  • Wood Treatments: Natural preservatives

4. Testing & Validation

  • Field trials in affected areas
  • Efficacy testing against local species
  • Safety testing for humans and pets
  • Environmental impact assessment

5. Production & Manufacturing

  • Small-scale production facility
  • Quality control procedures
  • Packaging design
  • Supply chain development

6. Marketing & Distribution

  • Target market identification (homeowners, farms, businesses)
  • Brand development
  • Online and retail distribution
  • Educational materials

7. Partnerships

  • Department of Agriculture
  • Local government units (LGUs)
  • Agricultural cooperatives
  • Environmental organizations

Deliverables

  • Product formulations
  • Manufacturing manual
  • Marketing materials
  • Sales platform
  • Training programs
