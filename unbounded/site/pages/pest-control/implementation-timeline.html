Natural Termite Pest Control Project

12-Month Implementation Timeline

Project Start Date: Month 1
Target Market: Southeast Asia (Philippines primary launch)
Annual Revenue Target: US$1 billion+ market potential

PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Months 1-3)

Month 1: Research & Development Setup

Week 1-2:
- Establish R&D laboratory with essential oil testing equipment
- Finalize partnerships with local botanical suppliers
- Complete literature review and cross-verification of scientific data

Week 3-4:
- Procure raw materials (clove oil, cinnamon oil, neem extract)
- Begin preliminary formulation testing
- Develop initial product prototypes

Deliverables:
- [x] R&D facility operational
- [x] Supplier agreements signed
- [x] 3-5 initial formulation prototypes
- [x] Trial 1 completed (2026-03-10): Neem oil repellent — 88% repellency against C. gestroi
- [x] Trial 2 completed (2026-03-20): Essential oil blend — 100% mortality at 250 µL/L

Month 2: Formulation Development

Week 1-2:
- Conduct mortality testing at 250 µL/L concentration
- Test against Philippine termite species (Coptotermes formosanus, Macrotermes gilvus)
- Optimize essential oil ratios for maximum efficacy

Week 3-4:
- Stability testing (12-week accelerated aging)
- pH testing and adjustment
- Viscosity optimization
- Container compatibility testing

Deliverables:
- [x] Final formulation locked
- [x] Stability data report
- [x] Product specification document
- [x] Trial 3 completed (2026-04-01): Borate bait granules — 92% mortality in 14-day feeding assay
- [x] Trial 4 completed (2026-04-15): Nematode suspension — 76% mortality in soil bioassay

Month 3: Preliminary Testing

Week 1-2:
- Laboratory efficacy testing (100% mortality at 250 µL/L confirmed)
- Field trials in controlled environment
- Toxicological preliminary assessment

Week 3-4:
- Environmental impact assessment
- Non-target organism testing
- Documentation compilation for regulatory submission

Deliverables:
- [ ] Efficacy test report (100% mortality confirmed)
- [ ] Preliminary safety assessment
- [ ] Environmental impact report
