TOXICOLOGICAL TESTING

Acute Toxicity Studies

Oral Toxicity

Study Species Dose Duration Cost
Acute Oral LD50 Rat 2000, 5000 mg/kg 14 days $8,000
Acute Oral LD50 Mouse 2000, 5000 mg/kg 14 days $8,000

Protocol:
1. Administer single dose via oral gavage
2. Observe for 14 days
3. Record mortality, clinical signs
4. Perform gross necropsy
5. Calculate LD50

Acceptance Criteria:
- LD50 > 2000 mg/kg = Category 4 (Low toxicity)
- LD50 > 5000 mg/kg = Category 5 (Very low toxicity)

Dermal Toxicity

Study Species Dose Duration Cost
Acute Dermal LD50 Rabbit 2000, 5000 mg/kg 14 days $10,000
Skin Irritation Rabbit Undiluted 72 hours $5,000

Protocol:
1. Apply product to shaved skin
2. Cover with occlusive dressing
3. Observe for 14 days
4. Record irritation, mortality
5. Calculate LD50

Acceptance Criteria:
- LD50 > 2000 mg/kg = Category 4
- Minimal to mild irritation acceptable

Eye Irritation

Study Species Dose Duration Cost
Eye Irritation Rabbit 0.1 mL 21 days $6,000

Protocol:
1. Instill product into conjunctival sac
2. Observe for 21 days
3. Grade irritation using Draize scale
4. Record reversibility

Acceptance Criteria:
- Minimal to mild irritation
- Full reversibility within 21 days

Inhalation Toxicity

Study Species Dose Duration Cost

Protocol:
1. Expose to aerosol/vapor for 4 hours
2. Observe for 14 days
3. Record mortality, clinical signs
4. Calculate LC50

Acceptance Criteria:
- LC50 > 5 mg/L = Low inhalation toxicity

Skin Sensitization

Guinea Pig Maximization Test

Study Species Dose Duration Cost
Sensitization Guinea Pig Induction + Challenge 6 weeks $10,000

Protocol:
1. Induction phase (intradermal + topical)
2. Rest period (10-14 days)
3. Challenge phase (topical application)
4. Score sensitization response

Acceptance Criteria:
- Sensitization rate < 15% = Weak sensitizer
- Sensitization rate < 1% = Non-sensitizer

Subchronic Toxicity

28-Day Repeated Dose

Study Species Dose Duration Cost
28-Day Oral Rat 0, 100, 500, 2000 mg/kg/day 28 days $25,000

Protocol:
1. Administer daily dose via oral gavage
2. Monitor clinical signs weekly
3. Body weight daily
4. Food consumption daily
5. Hematology at termination
6. Clinical chemistry at termination
7. Gross necropsy
8. Histopathology

Endpoints:
- Mortality
- Clinical signs
- Body weight
- Food consumption
- Hematology parameters
- Clinical chemistry
- Organ weights
- Histopathology

Mutagenicity Testing

Bacterial Reverse Mutation Assay (Ames Test)

Study Organism Dose Duration Cost
Ames Test S. typhimurium 5 doses + controls 2 days $5,000

Protocol:
1. Incubate bacteria with test article
2. Plate on selective media
3. Count revertant colonies
4. Compare to controls

Acceptance Criteria:
- Negative result (no mutagenic activity)

EFFICACY TESTING

Laboratory Efficacy

Contact Toxicity Test

Target Method Duration Cost
Coptotermes formosanus Direct contact 24-72 hours $5,000
Macrotermes gilvus Direct contact 24-72 hours $5,000
Reticulitermes speratus Direct contact 24-72 hours $5,000

Protocol:
1. Prepare test solutions at 250 µL/L
2. Expose termites to treated substrate
3. Record mortality at 24, 48, 72 hours
4. Calculate LT50

Acceptance Criteria:
- 100% mortality at 250 µL/L

Residual Efficacy Test

Target Method Duration Cost
Coptotermes formosanus Treated wood 30-180 days $8,000

Protocol:
1. Treat wood samples
2. Age samples for 30, 60, 90, 180 days
3. Expose to termites
4. Record mortality and wood damage

Acceptance Criteria:
- >90% mortality for 90 days
- >70% mortality for 180 days
