1. The Hook (30 seconds)

What if I told you the most destructive pest in the Philippines is actually our greatest untapped resource?

Three years of research. Ten experiments. And we have the data to prove it.

2. The Problem (1 minute)

A triple crisis: Energy (90% imported fuel), Chemical pesticides (₱12,000/hectare/season), and Protein (2.5M metric tons of imported animal feed).

3. The Solution — ITRI (2 minutes)

The Integrated Termite Research Initiative: one biological engine replacing fossil fuels, eliminating toxic pesticides, and feeding the nation.

4. The Technology (2 minutes)

Termite gut microbiome cellulolytic enzyme system for biofuel production; termite-derived biopesticides; termite-based protein meal for livestock.

5. Business Model & Ask

Phased commercialization with a clear ask for investment to scale the research and bring products to market.
