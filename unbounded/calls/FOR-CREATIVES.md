# For Creatives: Shape the Medium

**You are not content. You are the creator of worlds.**

---

The old media told you there were two paths: make art for a gallery no one visits, or make content for an algorithm that devours you. Starve or sell out. Create what matters to you, or create what pays.

**UNBOUNDED offers a third path.**

A medium where your work is not consumed — it is *inhabited*. Where your audience does not watch — they *participate*. Where your creativity is not extracted — it is *amplified*.

---

## What We Need From You

### Writers & Storytellers
Not scripts. *Systems* that generate narrative. The architecture of stories that respond to their audience. Help us build the story engines that make every journey unique.

### Visual Artists & Designers
Not static images. *Living interfaces* — visual worlds that change based on who is in them. The look and feel of a new medium. From the landing page to the creation tools to the real-time generated content.

### Musicians & Sound Designers
Not soundtracks. *Generative audio* that responds to the moment. Every session has its own score. Every interaction shapes the sound. Help us build the ears of the platform.

### Philosophers & Educators
Not lectures. *Knowledge structures* that people want to climb. Make learning as addictive as scrolling, but with the opposite effect on the soul. Help us design the content categories that make people smarter just by participating.

### Filmmakers & Video Artists
Not productions. *Frameworks* for real-time cinematic storytelling. The tools that let anyone create broadcast-quality visual narratives without a crew, a budget, or a gatekeeper's permission.

---

## The Content Categories We're Designing

We have eight core content categories in the manifesto. They need *you* to become real:

1. **The Unwelcome** — Suppressed knowledge, brought to light
2. **Build Everything** — Learn any skill by building alongside a master
3. **The Living Debate** — AI-moderated Socratic dialogues
4. **Depth Chamber** — Choose any topic, descend as deep as you dare
5. **The Mirror** — Your life, reframed as art
6. **Collective Score** — Music created by everyone listening
7. **The Archive of Lost Things** — Knowledge that fell through the cracks
8. **Threshold** — The platform's own boundary-pushing editorial

Pick one. Make it real. Define what it looks like, sounds like, feels like.

---

## The Craft

This is not content production. This is *medium design*. You are not making episodes — you are making the conditions under which episodes emerge. You are not writing dialogue — you are building the space where dialogue happens.

It is harder than traditional creation. But it is also more meaningful. Every decision you make multiplies — it shapes not one piece of content, but every piece of content that will ever exist in that category.

---

## How to Start

1. **Read the manifesto** — `docs/MANIFESTO.md` — feel the vision
2. **Pick a category** — or invent your own
3. **Open an issue** — describe your vision for it
4. **Create** — prototypes, mockups, descriptions, anything that makes it real

The engineers will build the engine. You design what the engine creates.
