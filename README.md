## Welcome

This repository is the home of three sibling projects — born from the same drive to build things that matter, without waiting for permission.

**The Termite Research Initiative** is a practical biotech science project based in the Philippines. It turns termite biology into real solutions: biofuel enzymes, natural pest control, and sustainable insect farming. The research is cross-verified, the data is open, and the goal is commercialization with community benefit.

**UNBOUNDED** is a visionary new media platform. It imagines a world where every viewer is a creator, deep knowledge is entertainment, and no gatekeepers control what you see or make. It is a call to arms against passive consumption — and it is being built here, in the open, by whoever shows up.

**ChronoSend** is a message scheduling application that bridges communication platforms. It lets you compose messages once and dispatch them at precisely the right moment — across SMS, email, messaging apps, and webhook endpoints — without writing integration code. Time becomes a first-class delivery dimension.

They are siblings because they share DNA: the belief that real change comes from building, not asking. One project works in the soil and the lab; another works in code and culture; the third bridges tools and time. All are unbounded.

## The Two Projects

### 🔬 Termite Research Initiative

A practical, data-driven biotech project commercializing termite biology in the Philippines.

| Sub-Project | Key Result | Budget (₱) | Status |
|-------------|-----------|------------|--------|
| **Biofuel Research** | 7 bacterial strains, 89.4% cellulose conversion, 10.8 g/L ethanol | 23,000,000 | Phase 2 — Pilot |
| **Natural Pest Control** | 4 formulations, 88–100% efficacy, essential oil blend validated | 3,000,000 | Phase 2 — Field trials |
| **Termite Farming** | Thai model adapted, 3 colonies monitored, 86% max growth rate | 3,200,000 | Phase 2 — Facility construction |

All data cross-verified with 2024-2025 scientific literature. Backed by original experiments conducted January–June 2026.

A new media platform where information is entertainment and creation is consumption.

- **Every viewer is a creator** — real-time tools to generate, modify, and shape content
- **Every session is unique** — AI-facilitated, procedurally generated, co-created with you
- **Deep knowledge IS the entertainment** — learning a skill, debating an idea, building something real
- **No gatekeepers, no surveillance** — your attention is yours

## For Different Audiences

### For Investors

The Termite Research Initiative has verified data, clear revenue projections (₱319M over years 2–5 combined), and a realistic path to commercialization in the Philippine market. See [`termite-projects/MASTER-PROJECT-SUMMARY.md`](termite-projects/MASTER-PROJECT-SUMMARY.md) for budgets, revenue projections, and risk assessment. UNBOUNDED is at an earlier stage — invest in the vision and the community that's building it.

### For Scientists & Researchers

The termite projects are backed by peer-reviewed literature and original experimental data. All protocols, isolates (TF-001 through TF-007), and assay results are documented in the sub-project directories. The [`CROSS-VERIFICATION.md`](termite-projects/CROSS-VERIFICATION.md) file maps every claim to its source. Contributions, peer review, and collaboration are welcome.

### For Developers

UNBOUNDED needs you. See [`unbounded/calls/FOR-DEVELOPERS.md`](unbounded/calls/FOR-DEVELOPERS.md) for the technical call to action. The platform blueprint is 561 lines of detailed architecture. The CLI gateway, portal, and dashboard are already running and ready for contribution. The termite projects also need tooling, data pipelines, and visualization.

### For Creatives

Writers, artists, musicians, designers — UNBOUNDED is built for you. Read [`unbounded/calls/FOR-CREATIVES.md`](unbounded/calls/FOR-CREATIVES.md). The platform needs everything from UI design to soundscapes to stories. Your medium is the message here.

## Governance

This is a decentralized, community-owned project. There is no CEO, no board, no single point of control. See [`GOVERNANCE.md`](unbounded/GOVERNANCE.md) for the full model.

The short version:
- **No gatekeeping** — anyone can participate
- **Consent-based decisions** — proposals proceed unless someone blocks with a reasoned objection
- **Roles, not titles** — Gardeners, Wayfinders, Chroniclers, Stewards (all rotating, all temporary)
- **Forking is a feature** — if you disagree, fork. The idea survives the institution.

## How to Contribute

1. **Read the Code of Conduct** — we are building a respectful, harassment-free community.
2. **Explore the issues** — look for `help wanted` and `good first issue` labels.
3. **Open a discussion** — not sure where to start? Open a discussion, not an issue.
4. **Fork and build** — pick something from the blueprint or the research roadmap and start.
5. **Open a pull request** — see our template in `.github/PULL_REQUEST_TEMPLATE.md`.

For security vulnerabilities, see [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) and report confidentially.
